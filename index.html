ABOUT US

Who we are

    Devices

    Band

    Our band is a unique development, thanks to which you will always be informed about the state of your health. Our band monitors temperature, SpO2, pulse. If it detects poor health, it sends health data to hospitals. It also advises which medicines to take, what see a doctor.

    ROBOT

    Robot

    The robot is a medical assistant. It helps medical workers in hospitals, or in your houses(now don). Although it can replace an experienced doctor by its functionality. What is the fate of our robot? The robot can move freely and due to this it can deliver necessary medications, medicines and many more to the hospital of another. The patient should name his health condition: temperature, pulse, pressure. Knowing your data, the robot will tell you which doctor should be consulted and which medications to take. The robot can measure temperature, pressure, pulse, all parameters of your health status. Get tips: which medicine to drink and others.

    APP

    Application

    Our application is an addition to our devices. The entire report on the state of your health is sent by the robot and the bracelet to the phone using our application. It is through the application that you can see your heart rate, temperature, pressure and many other indicators. Also, tips and recommendations will be displayed on the application .
