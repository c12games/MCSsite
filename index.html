    Robot

    Robot. For whom and what is it for?
    As we said earlier, our robot is designed for people with disabilities, patients of medical institutions and it is an excellent assistant for medical workers. The robot can perform its functions not only in medical institutions, hospitals and clinics, but also in an apartment or house. >Robot oportunities
    The name of the robot contains “Medical Assistant”. This means that the robot performs almost all the functions of a medical worker. The robot can dispense medication, medicine, and bulk medicine. You enter your health data (temperature, pressure, pulse) into the robot's program. The robot assesses your health and dispenses the right pills and the right bulk medicines. Also, if the robot detects poor health, it will send a signal to an ambulance. The robot calculates statistics about your health and if your condition worsens every day, after 3 days the robot will notify the hospital or your doctor about it. There is a help button at work. And if you suddenly feel sick, when you press a button, the robot calls an ambulance. You can also view your health status in a special application developed by us and on the Medical Care System website. A little about the system. Our system
    Our system represents the link between the robot and the rest of our developments. All information that the robot receives and measures about your health is synchronized with the application, the doctor and our website. Information about your health status (temperature, pressure, pulse, quantity and name of medications you took. All this can be seen in the application (which can be downloaded) and on the website (in the patient's personal account). Your doctor can also assess your condition and prescribe the necessary medications.Also, the doctor can go to the patient's personal account and see all the information about him.
