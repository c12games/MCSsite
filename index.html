ABOUT US

Who we are

We are the developers of MCS (Medical Care System). We are testing our first developments, an assistant robot and a device for measuring health. We are moving very fast. New ideas for developing our devices are constantly updated. The day is not far off when we launch our devices in mass production!

Our developments-our goals

Now we have developed 2 devices: an assistant robot and a smart bracelet for measuring health. On these devices there is a special application that we ourselves have developed, which receives data about your health using our devices.

    • Devices

    Band

    Our band is a unique development, thanks to which you will always be in the know about the state of your health. Our band controls temperature, SpO2, pulse. If he discovers poor health, he sends data about you to the hospital. It also advises which medicines to take and which doctors should be consulted.

    ROBOT

    Robot

    The robot is a medical assistant. Helps healthcare providers in hospitals or in your homes. The functionality of such an assistant can be replaced by an experienced doctor. So what does our robot do? The robot is free to move around the house or hospital. And thanks to this, he can deliver the necessary medicines and much more to the patient’s room. The patient must enter the data of his health condition: temperature, pulse, pressure. Knowing your data, the robot will tell you which doctor should be consulted and which medicines to take.


    APP

    Application

    Our application is an addition to our devices. The entire report on the state of your health is sent by the robot and the band to the phone using our application. It is through the application that you can see your heart rate, temperature, pressure and many other indicators. Also, tips and recommendations will be displayed on the application .
